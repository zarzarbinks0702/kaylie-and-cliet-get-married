The Big Event

With help from the Murder Mystery Company, we'll be solving a masquerade murder. This is an interactive event where guests will be chosen by the actors as suspects, and everyone must work together and interact to solve the mystery. Never been to a murder mystery party or don't know what it is? Click here to find out more about the company and what they do.

Need to Knows:

  • Where: Bouré Event Studio, 3033 W Parker Rd #201, Plano, TX 75023
  • When: November 4th, 2023, 5:30pm.
  • Dress Code: Casual. You can dress for a masquerade theme if you'd like (think casual gothic steampunk), but it's not required!
  • There will be no ceremony at this event.
  • This event is adults only. No one under 21 is allowed.
  • Dinner will be catered from Village Burger Bar Allen. Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options are available. Please specify dietary needs on your RSVP.
  • Masquerade masks will be provided for all guests to immerse everyone into the masquerade environment. Elastic strap and handle masks will be available.
  • No photographer will be at the event, but we encourage everyone to take pictures and upload them to this collaborative google photos album.
  • More details will be provided as we know more - check back often!
