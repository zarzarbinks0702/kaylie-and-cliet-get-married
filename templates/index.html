Our Story

We met in 2018 while working at AMC Stonebriar together. We began dating in December 2018. Through the years, we've had some awesome adventures together, from finding new parks to play Pokemon Go in, to going to Disney World, to surviving the lockdown together. We've been to many places across the country, including Orlando, El Paso, and Alaska, and even ventured international when we went to Vancouver. In May of 2022, we took a cruise through the Alaskan inside passage. While cruising around Hubbard Glacier, we got engaged! We have 2 cats - Kaylie brought Zari into the relationship, and in May 2019 we adopted Zephyr together. We're excited to get married and take on the next chapter of our lives together. We welcome you to join us for a unique reception as we celebrate #becomingthebutlers.
